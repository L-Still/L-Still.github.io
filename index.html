About Me

Hi, I’m Leslee Still, a data scientist fueled by curiosity, creativity, and a love of exploration. I thrive on uncovering patterns in data and building solutions that make a real impact.

I recently graduated summa cum laude with a B.S. in Data Science from Arizona State University. During my time there, I developed a strong foundation in analytics, coding, and communication, skills I now apply to meaningful, real-world projects.

My passion lies in machine learning, predictive modeling, and natural language processing. I especially enjoy creating visualizations that tell a compelling story and help others see the “why” behind the data.

I’m continually learning and growing in the data science field, currently focused on developing and implementing AI-driven solutions. I believe great insights come not just from powerful models, but from thoughtful questions and purposeful design.
